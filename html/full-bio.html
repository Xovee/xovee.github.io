Xovee Xu

Xovee Xu will be a Ph.D. student in computer science in Fall 2021 with the school of computer science and engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, Sichuan, China, advised by Fan Zhou and Jingkuan Song. He received the B.S. degree in software engineering from UESTC, in 2018, and the M.S. degree in software engineering from UESTC, in 2021. He was born in Zizhou, Yulin, Shaanxi, China, in 1996.

He is the author of several research articles published in INFOCOM, SIGIR, and CSUR. His research interests include social network data mining and knowledge discovery, primarily focuses on information diffusion in full-scale graphs, human behavior understanding, and spatial-temporal data modeling in various social and academic scenarios.

Xovee has served as reviewers for refereed conferences and journals including KDD, AAAI, TKDE, TNNLS, and TON. He is a member of the IEEE and ACM.

Xovee lives in Chengdu, China. He speaks Chinese (Mandarin & Northern Shaanxi Dialect) and English, and he is learning Esperanto as well. His Chinese name is 徐增 (which means slowly increasing).
